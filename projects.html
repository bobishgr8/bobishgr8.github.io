While studying at Potato Junior College, I had the opportunity to partake in the E-STaR research programme. Here I got to work with NUS Faculty of Engineering, Computer Engineering on a web based facial recognition system based on a hybrid algorithm utilizing both k-NN for classification as well as a CNN for feature extraction. This project won silver for the 2020 Singapore Science and Engineering Fair (SSEF) competition.

Focus

Main focus of the research project

The main end goal of the project is to make a web login form based on the facial recognition system that was developed during testing. To do this, we need to get an image from the user's webcam, take the image and send it to the server for further processing.

Development and testing

Various processes and steps taken

To arrive at the ideal algorithm, I had to test various approaches and algorithms. This is also in addition to tuning many parameters of the various algorithms that was used to achieve the ideal balance between performance and security.

Results and further development

End results and conclusion

The end goals was achieved. However, there remains further areas of research and improvements that can be worked on. One of them is low light performance. There can also be improvements in side pose facial detection to enable a more user friendly login.

AOR auto-fill project

python-mailmerge 0.22.2

In some schools in Singapore, there exists aa form known as the AOR form. Potato Junior College has to fill in a AOR form every time they need to buy something. I don't know what it stands for, they never told me. Nevertheless, I was tasked to automate the filling of all the AOR forms for all the departments. So I wrote a python script that took advantage of two python libraries, one to extract data from an excel sheet containing the data for the forms, and another to fill that into specific areas of the word document.
